About Hometown Diary

Hometown Diary's Overview

Hometown Diary is a 4 years 1 month old enterprise incorporated on 05-Mar-2021, with its registered office located at No.15/89B, Sunrise Avenue, Keelathirukazhukundram, Lakeview Road, Thirukazhukundram, Tamil Nadu. Our major activity lies in Manufacturing, sub-classified under the Manufacture of food products, primarily focused on the production of pasteurized milk — plain and flavored — delivered in eco-friendly packs and bottles.

Hometown Diary's Vision

At Hometown Diary, our core vision is to bridge the gap between small-scale local farmers and health-conscious consumers by offering natural, farm-fresh dairy products. We dream of kicking commercialization to the curb, delivering pure, preservative-free dairy goodness that's sustainable, affordable, and super fresh, straight from the heart of the farms.

Hometown Diary's History

Founded on March 5, 2021, Hometown Diary started its journey with a simple idea — to serve wholesome dairy to the community while uplifting the lives of local farmers in Tamil Nadu and beyond. From a humble beginning, we’ve grown into one of the fastest-rising names in the dairy industry.

Every day, we hustle hard to deliver quality that you can literally taste — while keeping our roots grounded in tradition and care.

  • 120+ customers served fresh every single day
  • 750 liters of milk sourced daily from passionate local farmers
  • Over 200 dairy farmers empowered and supported
  • 25+ neighborhoods across Chennai and surrounding towns served
  • Entrepreneurship wave: Dealers and small business partners emerging from within the community
  • Support and uplift over 150 farmers through direct partnerships
  • Launch new initiatives to strengthen the farming community with modern solutions

About Home Town Dairy Products

HTD Milk's mission is to deliver fresh dairy products to your doorstep, supporting local farmers and ensuring top-quality milk, ghee, and other dairy essentials.
